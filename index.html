Everything you need to know about A/B testing

A/B testing is a method of comparing two versions of a webpage, email, or other marketing asset to determine which one performs better. You split your traffic between the two versions and measure which one achieves your goal more effectively.

The duration depends on your traffic volume and the size of the effect you're testing. Generally, you should run tests for at least one full business cycle (typically 1-2 weeks) and until you reach statistical significance. Our platform automatically calculates when your test has enough data.
